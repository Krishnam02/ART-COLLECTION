Expanding Our Craft: From Events to Iron & Steel Manufacturing

At Art Collection, while we are renowned for our exquisite event management and design services, we also take pride in our craftsmanship when it comes to manufacturing high-quality iron and steel products. Our expertise extends beyond creating stunning wedding sets, as we also craft beautiful and durable products for homes, gardens, and commercial spaces.

Our range includes everything from elegant gates and railings to custom-made candle stands and flower pots. Whether you’re looking for functional pieces or decorative ironwork, we blend artistic creativity with industrial precision to bring your vision to life.

Iron Gates

Our iron gates are designed for both security and aesthetics. Handcrafted with care, they are durable and elegant, providing a grand entrance for homes and commercial properties.

Steel Railings

Our steel railings are known for their strength and modern designs. Whether for staircases, balconies, or outdoor use, our railings offer safety with a sleek, stylish appearance.

Candle Stands

Our candle stands are crafted to enhance the ambiance of any space. With a variety of designs ranging from classic to contemporary, they add a touch of elegance to both indoor and outdoor settings.

Flower Pots

Our flower pots are designed to be both functional and decorative. From simple to ornate, they are perfect for enhancing gardens, patios, and interiors.

Custom Ironwork

We offer custom ironwork solutions tailored to your unique requirements. From artistic creations to functional designs, our custom pieces blend form and function seamlessly.
